Ques: What is Mobbanner?
 Ans: Mobbanner is a Simple Android App, gives free mobile recharge to users for downloading offer apps.

Ques: How do I join Mobbaner?
 Ans: Mobbanner is zero cost, to be a member. All you have to do is install Mobbanner App from Google play store into your mobile device. Fill the fields and confirm with registered email address.

Ques: How do I earn free recharge from Mobbanner App?
 Ans: Just need to login into your Mobbanner account and install listed offers. After installing the app, Open the app screen for 3 sec.

Ques: How do I redeem my points?
 Ans: Go to balance tab, Input the number to recharge, Select the operator and enter the amount.

Ques: How do I delete my account?
 Ans: If you don't want to be a Mobbanner user, send an email remove@mobbanner.com

Ques: How to change the Password?
 Ans: Go to menu tab, click password then enter your new password.

Ques: What if I forget my password?
 Ans: If you don't remember your password, you can reset easily with simple steps. Go to home screen, click forgot password, then enter the associated email address. Follow the email instructions on changing the password.

Ques: My balance not updated,after installing?
Ans: If you have installed the listed offers in the past using same device, you won't get any points.
